For charters: what the trust flags promise
flagmeasured, held outwhat to do
HIGH 92% of flagged points need at most 5 fixes (44% of points flagged); only ~35% are within 2 fixes Start from the draft; verify every token, especially serve zones and endings
LOW no promise — 55% are 6+ fixes out Chart from scratch

Weakest feeds for the flag: clay RG-24 (57%) and AO night (76%) — their failure modes are invisible to the confidence signals. There is deliberately no sign-off tier: it has failed held-out validation at every attempted n.

Never submit an uncorrected draft to the Match Charting Project.
